Annotations can be found in many places throughout Lithium. The general principle behind Annotations is to provide a way for people to attach their own notes to things. Like digital sticky notes, they can be added to most anything. They are intended to be shared with anyone looking at the same sorts of things.

Most often, they are attached to records in a table. 

For example, in the Lookups table, we have a list of Lookups, and then when youlve selected a Lookup, you see its values. You can Annotate either the main Lookup in the first table, or an individual value in the second table. You might do this to post a reminder about why a certain Lookup has changed, or who to call when they want to know more about why a certain record has a certain value.

In terms of the bigger picutre, Annotations are intended as a way for an ogranization to capture more of the knowledge of its workers. Like sticky notes, there\'s a lot of value in what people know about what is going on beyond what is just stored in a database somewhere, and this is a way to help capture a little more of that knowledge.
