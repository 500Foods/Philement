This  course covers very nearly every conceivable thing anyone needs to know about how Lithium works and how to get the most from it. It was created as an alternative to things like PDFs and Word documents and Powerpoint slides, though you might just find some or all of those littered about in this course as well.

The course, like all courses here, is structured into a series of Modules, each focused on a particular task, function, or in the case of Lithium, a specific \"Manager\".  Many Lithium users will be tasked with working with one or more Managers as part of their daily routine. Few will need to understand how to use *all* of the Managers, however. So the course is open in that respect. Once you get through the initial Modules, you\'ll be free to learn about the Managers that  you actually plan on using, or would like to use.

The quizzes, aside from the introductory quiz, should be taken seriously. Some have been designed to serve as a prerequisite to getting access to a Manager, or at least access that permits you to make changes. In general, it would be good to have Lithium open in another browser tab as you work through the material so you can try out and explore alongside whatever the Module is covering.

The course isn\'t a fixed thing, however. We can make changes to it at any time, and absolutely improve it if you encounter a section that is unclear, incomplete, or perhaps out of sync with what is in the current version of Lithium that you\'re using. Or perhaps it could be better tailored to how you\'re using it in your organization. By all means fill out some of the feedback, participate or initiate discussions, or use the Concierge service within Lithium itself whenever you find the course material to be less than ideal.
