The Concierge feature is a bit like a ticketing system. You can ask questions, but it is people that will respond to it rather than Crimson, the AI built into Lithium. This is where you can make requests either for information about something you need help with, or to make suggestions or feature requests. These are generally intended to be private 1:1 conversations between you and the people supporting Lithium. This may include support people local to your organization or people on the Lithium development team. Please use your own judgement when it comes to sharing information in your request. Naturally there\'s little risk in posting tickets that support people in your organization will see. There may be policies in your organization that prohibit sharing certain kinds of information with people outside of your organization, however.

The Crimson interface has an option to create a Concierge Request from a conversation that youv\'e had. This is intended for when Crimson didn\'t do a good enough job or gave you bad advice, so that improvements can be made or to correct the knowledge that Crimson relies upon.
