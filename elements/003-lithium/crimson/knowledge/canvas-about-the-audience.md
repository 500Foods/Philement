Here are the kinds of people that this course was created for.

- **Lithium Users.** People who use Lithium on a day-to-day basis. They may use a branded version, like GLM or GAIUS or Acuranzo or others. And this course might be renamed and adjusted for their specific orgnaizational needs, but they are the people that use Lithium the most and might be looking for a bit of a leg up in getting more out of it.
- **Managers.** Many people use data that comes from Lithium without actually using Lithium directly. Published reports, e-mail notifications and more can all come out of Lithium without the recipients needing access to it directly. Typically this is the manager-types. This course might be very useful to them, particularly aspects related to the Dashboard Manager that they can use to get better information in a more timely fashion.
- **IT People.** If your job involves tech support or otherwise helping the people in your organization with their challenges, then getting up to speed on Lithium yourself might be a very good idea. 
- **Developers.** There are Modules later on that pertain to those who want to integrate or extend or otherwise use Lithium in different ways. This is very much a traditional client-server system at its heart, based on things like SQL and Lua scripts, JavaScript clients and a number of backend databases. So lots of flexibility and lots of things for a developer to sink their teeth into.
