In this module we\'re going to cover related projects in more detail. Some of these, like the Hydrogen and Helium projects, are key Lithium dependencies. Others are of interest because they contribute in someway to Lithium\'s development, or are in fact Lithium deployments that have been branded as something else and extended in new ways. Some are discussed as they give more of a feel for the environments that Lithium was designed to either operate within, augment, or replace entirely.
