Notifications are very much like traditional notifications - when Lithium detects that something has changed that you wish to be notified about, it registers a Notification for you. A counter appears when there are unread Notifications. What notifications you can receive are configured in the Profile Manager, which you can access with the button in the bottom left corner. 

Generally speaking, Notifications are generated when an event of some kind has occured.

- A record has changed since you last logged in
- You\'ve placed a watch on something, like in a table, so you will be notified when it is changed
- Someone has added to an Annotation that you\'ve previously contributed to

Clicking on the Notifications button will open up the Notifications Manager where you can see all of your Notifications, Watches and more.

Notifications are specific to you, and the things that you are Watching are also specific to you. Annotations, as we\'ll see in the next section, are (deliberately) visible to anyone in the organization who has access to whatever the annocation is attached to.
